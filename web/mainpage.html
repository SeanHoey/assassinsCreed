Assassin's Creed wiki


Assassins creed is a set of game that is set in different time and place over the world. The first game was released on 3 in November 2007 and the latest game has came on the 31 of october 2012. The game follows desmond as he is put into a machine that allows him to experience his ancestor memories, the player get to control the ancestor and do missions passed on the ancestors memory.
